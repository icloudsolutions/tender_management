ICS SMB Management

Implements the SMB Division Standard Operating Procedure (SMB-SOP-1) for Sales, Credit Control, Logistics, and Collection.

Features

  • Credit approval workflow on Sale Orders (Send to Credit → Approve / Reject)
  • Credit Control group and dedicated menu (Credit Approval queue)
  • Critical order flag for logistics coordination
  • Optional credit limit check on approval

Workflow

Sales sends quotation to Credit Control → Credit Control approves or rejects → After approval, order can be confirmed and processed via standard delivery and invoicing.
